What is Cloud Gaming?

The concept of cloud gaming is a relatively new technology in which the game engine processing happens on a powerful server and the video stream is sent to any computer using nothing other than the power of the internet. Cloud gaming is a streaming service that provides high-quality gaming content but has high demands of internet bandwidth and server capacity. Using any available device like laptop, pc, smartphone or a tablet you could stream a gameplay simultaneously at your home or wherever you are offer your max levels of conveniences. That means a your PC is no longer needed last technologies hardware.
